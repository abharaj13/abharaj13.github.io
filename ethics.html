IT Ethics

Ethical considerations in IT are crucial for responsible innovation and maintaining trust. Here are some key guidelines:

  • Follow the ACM Code of Ethics.
  • Ensure data privacy and security in all software development practices.
  • Adhere to transparency and fairness in AI and machine learning applications.

Citations:

  • Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). "Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct." Available at https://www.acm.org/code-of-ethics.
